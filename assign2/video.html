Guitar - A Cruel Angel's Thesis


A Cruel Angel's Thesis is a Japanese Animation - Neon Genesis Evangelion's theme song originally performed by singer Yoko Takahashi. The producer of the Neon Genesis Evangelion expended much care and thought on this theme song. Every single tone and rhythm reflects the society of Japan in the 1990s. This guitar version of A Cruel Angel's Thesis is performed by Daisuke Minamizawa.
