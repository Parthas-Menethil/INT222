Carrying You (Laputa)


Carrying You (Laputa) is the theme song of a Japanese Film – Castle in the sky directed by Miyazaki Hayao. The film express us the collapse of an empire because of power of politics in a young boy's and a young girl's view. The composer of this song – Jou Hisaishi injected the soul of the film into this song. He let people to recall their past and added a little sense of mourn in the harmonic theme. Inside the rhythm, it expresses the memory of the past adventure and the praise of innocence. The whole music permitted with the longing of the future and to go forward.
